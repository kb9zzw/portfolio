Hello and welcome to my GIS portfolio!

On this site, you will find a demonstration of my GIS capabilities as well as a showcase of some of my projects accumulated while pursuing a Master's degree in Geospatial Information Science and Technology at North Carolina State University. The goal of this site is to show a well-rounded sampling of my understanding of geospatial technologies and their application to environmental issues.

About Jon

My expertise extends beyond GIS and includes a strongly technical background in web development, systems engineering and scientific programming. I am attracted to projects that allow me to mix my interest in environmental science, specifically meteorology and climatology, with my skills in software development and systems engineering. I will be graduating in December, 2014. Afterwards, I'll be looking for ways to apply my technical and geospatial skills in new and interesting ways. If you'd like to suggest a GIS project or would just like to contact me, feel free to drop me a note.

You can also find me on LinkedIn.
